Welcome to the Living Green Club PAU

A community dedicated to promoting environmental awareness, sustainability, and conservation practices within Pan-Atlantic University. Join us in protecting our campus environment, one initiative at a time.

Join Now
View Activities

Our Mission

To educate, empower and mobilize PAU students around sustainability, ensuring every campus decision prioritizes environmental integrity, human health, and community well-being.

What We Do

Environmental Audits

Regular walk-through inspections of facilities to identify hazards and provide maintenance recommendations.

Hazard Management

Monitoring and controlling physical, chemical, biological & psychosocial risks on campus.

Awareness Campaigns

Leading initiatives like the Styrofoam ban and organizing educational pop-ups in the student centre.

Action Days

Organizing tree-planting events, campus clean-ups, and educational visits to wildlife sanctuaries.

Notable Projects

NCF Tree-Planting Exercise

Collaborated with NCF Green Recovery Nigeria to plant over 200 trees across campus, enhancing our green spaces and carbon absorption capacity.

PDS with Industry Leaders

Personal Development Series featuring Mrs. Odunayo Sanya (MTN Foundation) and Mrs. Dayo Aderugbo on environmental leadership.

Wildlife Sanctuary Visit

Educational field trip to Green Fingers Wildlife Sanctuary, learning about habitat protection and conservation efforts.
